# Super Store Sales Analysis Project

## Project Overview

This project presents a Power BI dashboard analyzing Super Store sales data to identify key performance drivers, uncover business opportunities, and support data-driven decision-making. The dashboard focuses on sales performance, customer behavior, and operational insights.

---

## Key Business Insights

### Market Performance

* Naypyitaw leads with **34.24%** of total revenue
* Mandalay and Yangon follow closely with **32.88% each**

### Seasonal Trends

* January shows the highest sales performance (**36.01%**)
* February records the lowest (**30.1%**)
* Indicates clear seasonal fluctuations in demand

### Customer Demographics

* Gender distribution is balanced:

  * Male: 48.02%
  * Female: 51.98%
* Membership distribution is also balanced:

  * Members: 50.85%

### Category Performance

* Top-performing categories:

  * Food & Beverages (highest sales)
  * Sports & Travel
  * Electronics

### Payment Preferences

* E-wallet: 34.5%
* Cash: 34.4%
* Credit Card: 31.1%

---

## Strategic Recommendations

* Focus marketing efforts on high-performing categories such as Food & Beverages
* Launch targeted campaigns in February to address seasonal sales drops
* Strengthen loyalty programs to increase member retention and revenue
* Encourage digital payment adoption to improve transaction efficiency
* Optimize inventory and operations in high-performing regions like Naypyitaw
